What is D&D?

D&D is a roleplaying game that is focused on storytelling. With D&D, you can go in multiple adventures filled with quests, battles, and treasures. The adventures are usually filled with magic, mysteries, and lots of fun. And the best part is that you have fun with friends. Do you want to know more?
